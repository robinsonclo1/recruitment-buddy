    Recruitment: Made Easy

    A month before recruitment, we'll send an online form for your members to fill out with their interests, activities, and hobbies. When trying to match members to PNMs, simply click the PNM's activities and a list of all members who share interests will appear. Select the member, assign them to the party, and move on. It's that easy.
